General Disclaimer

Colour Representation — Product colours displayed on our website, social media, and catalogues may appear slightly different in real life. Variations in screen calibration, ambient lighting, and photography conditions can affect colour accuracy. We strive to represent our jewellery as faithfully as possible.

Handcrafted Variance — Many pieces in the Tanushree Collection are handcrafted or hand-finished. Minor differences in shape, texture, weight, or finish are inherent characteristics of artisan craftsmanship — not defects. Each piece is unique.

Representational Imagery — All product images, lifestyle photographs, and campaign visuals are for representational purposes only. Styling, background props, and models are chosen for aesthetic presentation. The delivered product may differ in styling context.

§ 02 — Material Transparency

Product Authenticity

"We believe complete transparency is the highest form of luxury. Our material disclosures are crafted with the same care as our jewellery."

Fashion & Imitation

A portion of our catalogue features fashion jewellery, imitation jewellery, and premium-replica pieces. These products are not made of precious metals or certified gemstones unless explicitly stated in the product description.

Gold-Plated Products

Items described as "gold-plated," "gold-toned," or "gold-finish" contain a base metal with a gold-coloured coating. These products are not real gold and do not carry gold purity hallmarks unless the listing explicitly states otherwise.

Silver Replica

Products described as "silver-look," "silver-toned," or similar are fashion replicas. They are not pure silver or sterling silver unless the product listing carries an explicit silver purity mention or BIS hallmark.

Black Polish Jewellery

All black polish, oxidised, or matte-finish jewellery offered by Tanushree Collection is classified as fashion jewellery. These are not precious-metal products and are priced and presented as premium fashion accessories accordingly.
